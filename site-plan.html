Content

Home page

Header
This area will clearly guide you to the different areas/pages of the rest of the content. Having an accomplishments
button and maybe an explore tab and a contact me that has a dropdown with different options like my phone number,
email, social media, etc.

The main screen will be the main showcase of my accomplishments and/or work history. Here I will be able to display
the different school and personal projects that employers will be able to look at and go through so they can see what
I am capable of. I like the idea of having a "coming soon" area to show that I am currently working on more and have
plans to update the site with additional content. Languages I know. I will also add a way to view my Google calender
for availability here.

The Main page Stuff (from top to bottom)
• logo and nav bar on top with a dropdown menu for contact
• Header with my name and major/career/specialty
• the main page will have the languages I know, my skills, my best work, and a downloadable resume option
• I might put the resume in the footer so that way it is available on every page

Accomplishments/Goals

Accomplishments I want to either have an entire page dedicated to this or just have a sub-page inside of one of the mains that shows off both academic and nonacademic accomplishments and also have an area that displays the things I would like to accomplish or something that I am currently working on Goals I think the best area for goals would be to have a hover effect that shows the things that I am working towards, Academic and non-academic.

Meet Me/Bio

Here I will put random things about myself—the music I like, Where I grew up, stuff about my family, etc. Basically, this would be like the cover letter of a basic resume but also have a little bit of this info sprinkled around the entire site.
